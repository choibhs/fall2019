Todd Gurley breaks free for a big 133 yard game against the Bears



  • In recent months, there have been doubts of Todd Gurley's ability to perform with the Rams. He suffered a knee injury in the 2018 season, and could not get any momentum going into 2019. However, against the bears, he had a remarkable game, rushing for 133 yards in the Week 11 victory. His breakout game certainly raised the hopes of Gurley fans. The question remains though: Can he consistently perform like this as he was exspected to in his earlier years?
