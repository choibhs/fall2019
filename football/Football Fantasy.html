Fantasy Football






Background Info:
Fantasy football, like other fantasy games, puts you in the front office and on the sidelines as General Manager and Coach of your team. You select from the best of the best in the NFL and they compete on a weekly basis for your team. Their on-field performance drives your fantasy point total and overall success
Specifically, fantasy football works like this: You decide what type of league you want to participate in, acquire a roster of players (either through a draft or through autopick assignment), then set your lineup each week during the season and watch as touchdowns, field goals, yards gained, sacks, interceptions and much, much more fantasy points are scored for or against your team.
