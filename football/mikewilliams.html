Mike Williams leaps up to catch a 50 yard pass in an epic loss to the chiefs




Towards the end of the game in a Chargers v. Chiefs showdown, Mike Williams launched himself into the air, catching a 50 yard pass by Phillup Rivers. As their hopes of winning the game were slipping, down 24-17 with one minute lef, the Chargers were in desperate need of a huge. While Mike Williams pulled off this stunning, miracle catch, giving his team a chance to score in the redzone, the Chargers were unable to pull it off. Young, rookie quarterback, Patrick Mahomes was proven to be just too much, even against eccentric plays like this.
