Louisiana State University (LSU)



Team



LSU is stacked this year and thanks to a breakout season by Quarterback Joe Burrow (pictured left) they have had an undefeated season and are Number one on the AP Rankings. After dismantling Georgia, they will be facing off against Oklahoma in the Peach Bowl and Joe Burrow proved himself to be the best player in the nation.

Stadium




They have been playing home games at Tiger Stadium or "Death Valley" since 1924 and since then they have been thought of as one of, if not the loudest stadiums in the country. In 2001, ESPN sideline reporter Adrian Karsten said, "Death Valley in Baton Rouge is the loudest stadium I've ever been in."
